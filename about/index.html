ABOUT

Built as objects, not decoration.

Each piece defines the space it enters.

ECNA works between object and architecture.

From object to interior.

ECNA creates sculptural pieces and complete living spaces with the same architectural discipline.

Material.
Proportion.
Light.
Silence.

Form is reduced until only presence remains.

Nothing is added for effect.

Nothing remains without consequence.

Mass, edge, surface, and silence become the language.

The result is not furniture in the ordinary sense.

It is a spatial position.

Material is not a finish.

It carries memory.

Stone carries weight.

Leather carries time.

Metal carries tension.

Production is limited by intention.

Presence cannot be multiplied without losing force.

ECNA does not fill spaces.

It defines them.

Between silence and structure.

Between restraint and intensity.

Between object and architecture.
